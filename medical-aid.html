Know your costs before the day arrives

Medical aid cover depends on your scheme, your plan, and the procedure code. We explain what is paid, what needs authorisation, and what — if anything — you settle yourself. No guessing on the day.

  • Scheme acceptance

    Whether Florida Day Hospital and your chosen specialist are recognised by your medical aid.

  • Pre-authorisation

    Whether your procedure requires pre-approval, and who submits it on your behalf.

  • Your portion

    Any co-payment, shortfall, or self-payment — explained before you commit to a date.
