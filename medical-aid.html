Medical aids & payment

Understand your cover before your procedure

Your cover depends on your medical aid scheme, your plan, and the procedure you need. Before your appointment, we help you understand what is covered, what requires authorisation, and what — if anything — you may need to pay yourself. Clear answers upfront, so there are no surprises on the day.

Confirming your medical aid cover

When you book your procedure, our admissions team will guide you through scheme acceptance, pre-authorisation requirements, and any co-payments that may apply to your plan.

A list of accepted medical aid schemes will be published here as it is confirmed. If you are unsure whether your scheme is accepted, please contact us and we will assist.
