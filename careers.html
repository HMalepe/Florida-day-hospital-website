Careers

Work where every day has a purpose

Florida Day Hospital is assembling a team for people who thrive in focused, well-run clinical environments — where theatre lists keep time and every patient leaves the same day.

Day hospital nursing is not ward nursing. It is preparation, precision in theatre support, and recovery with a clock on the wall — because someone is going home tonight. If that pace and purpose suit you, we would like to hear from you.

Roles we commonly recruit for

Registered Professional Nurse — Day Theatre — scrub and circulating support for scheduled surgical lists.

Admissions Coordinator — the first voice patients hear; booking, authorisation, and calm coordination.

Patient Care Assistant — bedside support from arrival through to discharge.

Open positions will be posted here as they arise. Until then, submit a general enquiry and tell us what draws you to day hospital work.
