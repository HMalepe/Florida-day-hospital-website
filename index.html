About the Hospital

Care that is precise, personal and unhurried

Florida Day Hospital was built on a simple conviction: that surgical excellence and a calm, human experience are not competing priorities. Our theatres are equipped to full acute-hospital standard, while our scale allows every patient to be known by name.

Because we focus exclusively on planned day procedures, your surgery starts on time, your care team is dedicated to you, and your recovery is not interrupted by the noise of a large acute facility.

  • Fully licensed day hospital with specialist anaesthetic cover on every list
  • Dedicated pre-admission clinic — paperwork and screening done before the day
  • One nurse accompanies you from admission through to discharge
  • Follow-up call from your recovery nurse within 24 hours of surgery

Your Visit

A calm, predictable day — from arrival to discharge

Pre-admission

Screening, medical aid authorisation and all paperwork completed at our pre-admission clinic — before the day of surgery.

Admission

A short, quiet check-in. Your dedicated nurse meets you at reception and stays with you through preparation.

Your specialist and anaesthesiologist perform your procedure in a fully equipped modern theatre.

Monitored recovery, clear written aftercare instructions, and a follow-up call from your nurse within 24 hours.
