About us

A day hospital for the West Rand

Florida Day Hospital sits in Florida Park, Roodepoort — a private facility purpose-built for one kind of care: procedures that arrive, happen, and finish within the same day.

We exist because not every procedure needs an overnight bed. For cataracts, endoscopy, ENT surgery, gynaecology, general day cases, and pain intervention, the right setting is a hospital designed around a single rhythm — not a ward you sleep in.

What we stand for

Precision — theatre lists run to schedule. Your time is respected.

Dignity — calm spaces, clear communication, privacy where it matters.

Quiet excellence — the work speaks for itself. No fanfare, just care done properly.

Hospital Manager Yonela Tenza leads daily operations, with clinical teams staffed in partnership with Exclusive Medical Agency. Surgical care is delivered by visiting specialists — qualified physicians who perform procedures here on scheduled theatre days.

Whether you are referred by your GP, coming through your medical aid, or enquiring on behalf of a family member, you will find a hospital that treats your day as something complete — with a beginning, a middle, and a dignified end at home.
