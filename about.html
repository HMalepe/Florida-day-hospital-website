About us

Where surgical care is measured in hours, not days

Florida Day Hospital is a private day hospital in Florida Park, Roodepoort — purpose-built for scheduled procedures that are completed and discharged within a single visit.

Day surgery changes the experience of hospital care. Instead of an extended admission, your visit follows a defined pathway: arrival, preparation, your procedure in a dedicated theatre, monitored recovery, and discharge home — typically the same day.

Our hospital is managed by Yonela Tenza, with clinical teams staffed in partnership with Exclusive Medical Agency. Surgical and procedural care is delivered by visiting specialists across ophthalmology, gastroenterology, ENT, gynaecology, general surgery, and pain intervention.

Our aim is straightforward: to be a hospital where people feel safe, respected, and genuinely cared for — where every patient's day moves with purpose, from the first greeting to the final discharge.
