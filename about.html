About us

A hospital where your day has a clear beginning and end

Florida Day Hospital is a modern private day hospital in Florida Park, Roodepoort — built around scheduled, same-day surgical care with you home by evening.

We provide a single admission, a dedicated theatre, and a coordinated pathway from arrival through preparation, your procedure, monitored recovery, and same-day discharge.

Our team is led by Hospital Manager Yonela Tenza, with clinical staffing in partnership with Exclusive Medical Agency. Procedures are performed by visiting specialists across ophthalmology, gastroenterology, ENT, gynaecology, general surgery, and pain intervention.

To be a hospital where people feel safe, respected, and genuinely cared for — with every patient's day moving exactly as planned.
