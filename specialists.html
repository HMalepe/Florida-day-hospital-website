Our team

Specialists who operate here — on scheduled theatre days

Florida Day Hospital is a day-facility, not a consulting rooms. Visiting specialists perform procedures here on planned theatre lists — and we confirm yours when you book.

How specialist care works here

1 · Referral or enquiry — your GP or specialist recommends a procedure, or you contact us directly to begin.

2 · Booking — we match you to the right discipline and confirm your physician and theatre date.

3 · Procedure day — your specialist performs the operation here; our nursing team manages preparation and recovery.
