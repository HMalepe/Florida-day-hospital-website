Our team

The people who perform your procedure

Florida Day Hospital works with visiting specialists across six disciplines. Each procedure is performed by a qualified specialist on a scheduled theatre day — our admissions team confirms who will be treating you.

Yonela Tenza

Hospital Manager

Yonela keeps the hospital moving with purpose — coordinating theatre lists, nursing teams, and admissions so every patient's day proceeds with clarity and calm, from arrival through to discharge.

Staffed in partnership with Exclusive Medical Agency.

Visiting specialists by discipline

Imagery below is representative of each discipline. Your admissions team will confirm the specialist assigned to your procedure when you book.

  • Ophthalmology

    Cataract and eye day surgery on scheduled theatre days.

  • Gastroenterology

    Gastroscopy, colonoscopy, and digestive investigations under sedation.

  • ENT surgery

    Ear, nose, and throat day procedures for adults and children.

  • General surgery

    Hernia repair, lump excision, and minor general day surgery.

We also work with visiting specialists in gynaecology and pain management. A full directory with names and credentials will appear here as profiles are confirmed.
