Florida Day Hospital works with visiting specialists across multiple disciplines. Each procedure is performed by a qualified specialist on a scheduled theatre day — our team will confirm the right physician for your care.

How our specialist model works

Rather than a single in-house team, Florida Day Hospital hosts visiting specialists who perform scheduled procedures on dedicated theatre days. This model gives patients access to experienced physicians across a range of disciplines — with the hospital providing nursing care, theatre facilities, and admissions support throughout your visit.

  • 01

    Ophthalmology

    Eye & vision

    Cataract surgery and other ophthalmic day procedures, performed by visiting eye specialists on scheduled theatre days.

  • 02

    Gastroenterology

    Digestive health

    Gastroscopy, colonoscopy, and related endoscopic procedures under sedation, with same-day discharge.

  • 03

    ENT surgery

    Ear, nose & throat

    Day-case ENT procedures for adults and paediatric patients, scheduled with appropriate theatre and recovery time.

  • 04

    Women's health

    Diagnostic and minor gynaecological procedures in a private, professionally staffed clinical environment.

  • 05

    General surgery

    Day surgery

    Hernia repair, lump excision, and other minor general surgical procedures without an overnight admission.

  • 06

    Pain management

    Intervention procedures

    Image-guided pain intervention procedures, coordinated with your referring physician.
