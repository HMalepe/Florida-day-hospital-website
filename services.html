Your concerns

Care that starts with what brings you in

Every service at Florida Day Hospital is a scheduled day case — planned in advance, performed with dedicated theatre time, and completed without an overnight stay.

  • 01

    Cataracts & eye procedures

    Ophthalmic day surgery

    Cataract surgery and other ophthalmic day procedures — restoring clarity in one planned visit, with your specialist guiding what to expect afterwards.

  • 02

    Digestive & stomach investigations

    Gastroscopy · Colonoscopy

    When reflux, discomfort, or screening needs investigation, gastroscopy or colonoscopy under sedation allows you to return home the same day once recovery is complete.

  • 03

    Ear, nose & throat concerns

    ENT day surgery

    From recurring infections to airway and hearing concerns — ENT day procedures scheduled with the theatre time and recovery your case deserves.

  • 04

    Women's health procedures

    Gynaecological day surgery

    Diagnostic and minor gynaecological procedures in a discreet setting — your specialist leads every step, with privacy respected throughout.

  • 05

    General surgical procedures

    General day surgery

    Hernia repair, lump excision, and other minor general surgery — completed in a single visit, without an overnight hospital stay.

  • 06

    Ongoing pain management

    Pain intervention procedures

    Targeted, image-guided procedures for persistent pain — planned with your referring doctor and performed as a scheduled day case.

Procedures depend on visiting specialist schedules. Contact us before travelling so we can confirm your appointment.
