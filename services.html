Our services

Six disciplines, one same-day visit

From the concern that brings you in to discharge at home — every procedure below is planned as a day case, with dedicated theatre time and no overnight stay.

What your day looks like

Before — your referring doctor or specialist confirms the procedure and sends you to us. We handle booking and, where needed, medical aid authorisation.

On the day — you arrive at the agreed time, are prepared for theatre, undergo your procedure, and recover under nursing supervision.

After — once your specialist is satisfied, you are discharged home the same day with aftercare guidance from your physician.

  • 01 Cataracts & eye procedures Ophthalmic day surgery
  • 02 Digestive investigations Gastroscopy · Colonoscopy
  • 03 Ear, nose & throat ENT day surgery
  • 04 Women's health procedures Gynaecological day surgery
  • 05 General surgical procedures General day surgery
  • 06 Pain management Pain intervention procedures

Hover a service

Cataracts & eye procedures

Cataract surgery and other ophthalmic day procedures — restoring clarity in one planned visit, with your specialist guiding what to expect afterwards.

Digestive & stomach investigations

When reflux, discomfort, or screening needs investigation, gastroscopy or colonoscopy under sedation allows you to return home the same day once recovery is complete.

Ear, nose & throat concerns

From recurring infections to airway and hearing concerns — ENT day procedures scheduled with the theatre time and recovery your case deserves.

Women's health procedures

Diagnostic and minor gynaecological procedures in a discreet setting — your specialist leads every step, with privacy respected throughout.

General surgical procedures

Hernia repair, lump excision, and other minor general surgery — completed in a single visit, without an overnight hospital stay.

Ongoing pain management

Targeted, image-guided procedures for persistent pain — planned with your referring doctor and performed as a scheduled day case.

Theatre days vary by discipline and specialist. Enquire before you travel and we will confirm your appointment.
