What we treat

Care organised around your concern

Every procedure below is performed as a scheduled day case — admitted, treated, and discharged within the same day.

  • 01

    Cataracts & eye procedures

    Ophthalmic day surgery

    Clouded or deteriorating vision assessed and treated in a single scheduled visit.

  • 02

    Digestive & stomach investigations

    Gastroscopy · Colonoscopy

    Persistent reflux, discomfort or screening — investigated under sedation, home the same day.

  • 03

    Ear, nose & throat concerns

    ENT day surgery

    Recurring infections, blocked airways and hearing concerns, for adults and children.

  • 04

    Women's health procedures

    Gynaecological day surgery

    Diagnostic and treatment procedures performed with privacy and specialist-led aftercare.

  • 05

    General surgical procedures

    General day surgery

    Hernias, lumps, and minor surgical concerns treated without an overnight stay.

  • 06

    Ongoing pain management

    Pain intervention procedures

    Targeted, image-guided procedures for persistent pain, coordinated with your referring doctor.

Procedure availability depends on visiting specialists' schedules — please call to confirm your procedure before travelling.
