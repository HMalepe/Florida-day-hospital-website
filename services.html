What we treat

Procedures organised around what you need

Every service at Florida Day Hospital is delivered as a scheduled day case — planned in advance, performed with dedicated theatre time, and completed without an overnight stay.

  • 01

    Cataracts & eye procedures

    Ophthalmic day surgery

    Restore clarity and comfort with cataract surgery and other ophthalmic procedures, completed in one planned visit with specialist-led aftercare guidance.

  • 02

    Digestive & stomach investigations

    Gastroscopy · Colonoscopy

    Investigate reflux, abdominal discomfort, or screening needs with gastroscopy or colonoscopy under sedation — with recovery monitored before you leave.

  • 03

    Ear, nose & throat concerns

    ENT day surgery

    From chronic infections to airway and hearing concerns, ENT day procedures are scheduled with the time and attention your case requires.

  • 04

    Women's health procedures

    Gynaecological day surgery

    Diagnostic and minor gynaecological procedures in a discreet, professionally staffed environment — with your specialist guiding every step.

  • 05

    General surgical procedures

    General day surgery

    Hernia repair, lump excision, and other minor general surgical procedures — performed and completed without an overnight hospital stay.

  • 06

    Ongoing pain management

    Pain intervention procedures

    Targeted, image-guided pain intervention procedures for persistent discomfort — coordinated with your referring physician and recovery plan.

Availability varies by visiting specialist schedule. Please contact us before travelling to confirm your procedure and appointment time.
